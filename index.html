Our Bucketlist

Dreams to travel the world

Why We Want to Travel the World

There are many reasons we want to travel the world and experiene unique nature and cultures together.

Learn Different Languages

Ever since taking high school Spanish, I have always wanted to learn a new language. My husband also speaks Italian from his mission, which inspires me to learn a language on my own someday. I hope I can eventually learn one enough to use it in another country that we visit.

Build New Relationships

The world is full of so many different cultures, and my husband and I would love to experience them and learn about them. We want to meet people from other countries and learn about them and their ways of life.

See The World

My husband and I know there are so many beautiful places around the world, and we want to see them all. There is so much unique architecture and nature that we want to see. We absolutely love nature, and we can't wait to visit all of the diverse kinds of nature all over the world.

Protect Our Planet

We love our planet and the wildlife it provides refuge for. It is important to us that these animals stay safe and their homes preserved so we can enjoy them during our lifetime and others can enjoy them after us.
